How It Works

Expected Value (EV) — The average profit per $1 wagered. Positive EV means the bet has a mathematical edge.

Kelly Fraction — The optimal percentage of your bankroll to wager. Uses the Kelly Criterion formula: f* = (p × b − (1−p)) / b

Edge vs Book — The difference between your estimated probability and the bookmaker's implied probability.
